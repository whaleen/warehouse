DESCRIPTION OF MEET TRUCK MENU OPTIONS
 
WORKING - MT Orders in Process List of all meet truck orders which aren't ready to schedule with customer. These are potential future orders.
 
PENDING - MT Contact Customer List of all meet truck orders ready for you to contact customer to confirm the delivery. Contains link to Notify Logistic Team to request delivery, move the delivery date or cancel.
 
SHIPPED - MT Delivery Complete List of all meet truck orders shipped by the ADC but needing to be submitted through Check IN to bill customer and pay the Delivery Agent. Contains the ability to report late trucks, receiving report and carrier details.
 
ALL This option will let you review all meet truck orders in regardless of status.
 
PENDING - Multi-Family SDS Orders List of all Pending Multi-Family SDS Delivery orders.
